\section{Results}

We project the combined CLAS, VEPP-3, and OLYMPUS beam-charge data onto a common $(\varepsilon,Q^2)$ binning and fit the binned observable $\delta_{\mathrm{odd}}=(1-R)/(1+R)$ with low-rank models.

For the rank-$1$ hypothesis $\delta_{\mathrm{odd}}(\varepsilon,Q^2)\approx a(Q^2)b(\varepsilon)$, the weighted least-squares fit yields $\chi^2=2.48$ for $5$ degrees of freedom (reduced $\chi^2=0.496$), with relative $\ell_2$ residual 0.328.

Allowing rank-$2$ structure reduces the fit statistic to $\chi^2=2.63e-14$ for $-3$ degrees of freedom (reduced $\chi^2=NA$), corresponding to $\Delta\chi^2=2.48$ for $\Delta\nu=8$.

Figure~\ref{fig:rankfits} shows the observed binned $\delta_{\mathrm{odd}}$ together with the rank-$1$ and rank-$2$ reconstructions and residuals. Table~\ref{tab:rankk} summarizes the model comparison.
